Change Log
Mod List
Auto Cookie
A mod filled with settings that gives the game the power to fully play by itself. 
Game.LoadMod('http://trigger-death.github.io/TriggerCookies/Scripts/AutoCookie.js');

Auto Clicking

Automatically click popups such as Golden Cookies and Wrinklers. As features customizable speed for clicking the big cookie.

Auto Buying

Automatically buy buildings, upgrades, research, toggleables. Autobuy will pick out the most efficient item to buy next.

Season Cycle

Because ascending is so common in the beta version, the season cycle was introduced. It goes through every season until each season powerup has been collected.

Auto Ascend

Set multiplier settings to help the game decide when to ascend next. When ascending, the mod will buy the best upgrades first and spend a set percentage of heavenly chips on cookies. Before ascending the game will pop up with a 60 second prompt to choose to auto ascend, manually ascend, or disable the feature.
Enhance Cookie
A mod for modifying and improving the game interface to make the experience easier and more enjoyable. 
Game.LoadMod('http://trigger-death.github.io/TriggerCookies/Scripts/EnhanceCookie.js');

Improved User Interface

Has settings to remove the top bar, change the news ticker background, and setup improved scrollbars that don't scroll the news ticker. Also fixes a problem that causes the cookie count to flicker.

Helpful Buttons

Adds buttons for popping all wrinklers, ending the current season (at a price), and baking all heavenly chips.

Building and Upgrade Efficiency

Colors buildings and upgrades to show the most efficient one to buy next.
Stat Cookie
A mod will with tons of statistics that basically removes the need for the default stats page. 
Game.LoadMod('http://trigger-death.github.io/TriggerCookies/Scripts/StatCookie.js');

Cookie Stats

Shows improved CPS calculations as well as CPS from clicking and clicks per second.

Golden Cookie Stats

Shows tons of information about about golden cookies, their effects, and how to maximize their returns.

Prestige

Shows improved Heavenly Chip stats such as Chips per second, cookies per chip, and cookies gained from the next ascend.

Seasons

Keeps track of the progress made on each season, making it easier to decide when to switch to the next one.
Hotfix Cookie
Privides numerous togglable fixes for the beta version. 
Game.LoadMod('http://trigger-death.github.io/TriggerCookies/Scripts/HotfixCookie.js');

Fix Upgrades and Achievements

Fixes upgrades and achievements that either can't be unlocked, have incorrect requirements, or don't work properly.

And Other Miscellaneous Fixes

Fixes certain minor issues as well as an option to set heavenly cookie multipliers to their proper 1% value.
Cheat Cookie
Provides numerous features for hacking in the game. This mod is also exceptionally useful for debugging. 
Game.LoadMod('http://trigger-death.github.io/TriggerCookies/Scripts/CheatCookie.js');

Cookies, HChips, and HCookies

Allows you to add, set, remove, or multiply the amount of cookies, chips, and heavenly cookies in your game.

Achievements and Upgrades

Allows you to win, lose, buy, sell, lock, or unlock achievements an upgrades by typing in their name.

Spawning

Allows you to spawn popups such as Golden Cookies, Wrinklers, and Reindeer.
